About Lillah Services

Lillah Services, Inc. is recognized by the IRS as a not for profit 501c3 charitable organization. Your donation to Lillah Services is tax deductible to the extent permitted by law.

Lillah Services Inc tax identification number is 86-2695057

Our Mission

Serving the underprivileged with critical medical care, access to education and providing food insecurity assistance so that everyone can live full and dignified lives.

Our Vision

We believe that everyone should have access to critical medical care, free or affordable education and no one should sleep hungry.

Our Values

Access

We believe that everyone should have access to critical medical care, free or affordable education and no one should sleep hungry.

Dignity

We strive to ensure dignity for everyone that Lillah Services supports. We will never commercialize their situation and only use their information for the purposes of fundraising and never without their consent.

Transparency

We maintain transparency for every single transaction that comes through Lillah Services. Our donors expect that their donations are directed properly in the most efficient manner and we are committed to keeping their trust.
