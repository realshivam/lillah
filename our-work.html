Critical Care Action

We serve individuals who do not receive government assistance or have families that can afford to provide for treatment. In many cases, recipients are way below the poverty line and have to make trade offs that have long term impact and even mortal consequences. In some cases, medicine worth a few dollars is needed and in other cases, patients need immediate life saving amputation or dialysis. No two cases are ever similar and the team at Lillah Services works with each patient throughout and post treatment to ensure the highest rate of success. Oftentimes, patients go on to live full lives.

Education

Education is key to fighting poverty. In many impoverished communities, access to education is not within reach and far too often, parents have to choose to pay school fees or feed the family. When you make a gift to our education programs, you give a child hope for a brighter future.

High school students

They lost their father and unfortunately they didn’t have enough money to pay to school for their transfer script so they can go to college. Keeping their grades in mind, Alhamdulillah Lillah Services paid in full so they can continue their education. May Allah SWT give them success in every step of their life.
